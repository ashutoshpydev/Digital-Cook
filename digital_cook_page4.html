Ingredients

  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha hot sauce
  • 9 ounces soba noodles

Directions

  1. Step1: Combine broth, water, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, and hot sauce in a pot; bring to a boil.

  2. Step2: Add noodles to boiling broth mixture and cook until noodles are tender yet firm to the bite, 5 to 7 minutes.
