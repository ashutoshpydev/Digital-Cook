Black Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1 Tsp Instant Coffee Powder
  • 1 Cup Water
  • Sugar As Per Taste

Directions

  1. Step1: In a pan, add water and bring to boil.

  2. Step2: In a cup, add instant coffee powder and sugar. You can skip the sugar if you want a sugarfree drink.

  3. Step3: Next add the boiling water to the cup.

  4. Step4: Stir well. Enjoy!
